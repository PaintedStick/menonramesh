Ramesh Menon, Director, Certes Realty Ltd.

Ramesh Menon is a strategy & distribution development professional with more than 3 decades of experience. He’s had business development & leadership stints in organizations like Godrej & Boyce, MRF Ltd, Goodyear Tires, Ventura Logistics, BG Telecom India, Certes Realty Ltd & Longman Publishing.

He has also advised multiple startups and mid-size organizations on Corporate Strategy & execution of branding, marketing & distribution development. He is currently the President of Dr. Kessler's I2 labs and works with Dr. Jack Kessler, foremost amongst Iodine scientists in the world, to develop formulations and products in the war against pathogens. Ramesh would develop & execute the strategy of establishing the company in India.

He’s also on the founder team of Certes Realty Limited; a boutique organization with the core competence in Land in the Delhi NCR region, Urbanization and Delhi Master Plan (MPD 2041). He has been involved with multiple projects on a 'concept to creation to delivery', including business strategy, planning, funding, branding, marketing & sales. Ramesh continues to assist policy makers, at multiple levels, with issues pertaining to Land, law-making & urbanization, with a view to propagate development through collective benefit for all stakeholders, including LandPooling, Green development and such innovative mechanisms.

Ramesh has delivered distribution development assignment for startup brands including Data driven tech products by successful placement into the TG by developing & executing the brand communication & value benefits strategy.

Ramesh has also served as an independent director on the board of startups & listed companies.

Ramesh was also closely involved the with Prime Minister’s ambitious policy of ‘Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana’ (SAGY), and had adopted a village in NW-Delhi, with the then Member of Parliament. He has worked closely with the Delhi Urban Arts commission (DUAC) in developing the first Integrated development plan for a village, in India. While advising a financial services company, Ramesh ideated for the planning, raising capital and implementation of some tenets of the ambitious ‘Smart cities’ project.

Ramesh is a keen contributor to the media as an empaneled panelist with mainline TV channels like NDTV, CNBC, TimesNow etc. He also contributes articles & insights to mainline print publications. Ramesh is also an invitee to many forums & seminars as a speaker and a guest faculty in educational institutions

Ramesh’s Blogs & websites have had more than 2 million readerships over the past years and are key resource for many industry peers & researchers.

An avid outdoor person, Ramesh also is involved with multiple CSR & philanthropic ventures.
